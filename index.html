Software Engineer

Vacancy: 5

Do you want to deliver the best lines of code you can write so far? Do you want to explore professional grade working experience and be a part of a thriving software company?
In our development team, you will design and implement software components to enable and optimize our existing code repositories and upcoming projects.

Responsibilities

Your role as an engineer, you will work on real-world problems and build the most efficient way to solve them. You will work as a team. You may lead a team of engineers and software architects, giving thoughtful feedback, and creating a creative and rigorous engineering environment. Or work as a software architect. An entrepreneurial, start-up approach and willingness to take end-to-end ownership are key, as is the ability to truly partner and collaborate across organizations.

Qualifications

Required

  • Good understanding about OOP
  • Ability to write clean code
  • Good understanding about MVC

Preferred

  • Familiar with .NET ecosystem
  • Good understanding about SOLID design principal
  • Familiar with AGILE, SCRUM and DevOps tools

Working hours

On-demand working hours. That means you have to work as many hours as needed to meet the deadline.

Salary

24 BDT/y with stock options.

Note: You will have to adopt multiple languages, frameworks, tools and technologies after joining.
